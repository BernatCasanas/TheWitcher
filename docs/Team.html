We Are Magical Muffin

About Us

Magical Muffin is the team that has created The Witcher: A Bard’s Tale.
This team was created for the college subject called “Project 3” of the Bachelor’s Degree in Video Game Design and Development at the UPC. Based in Terrassa, Barcelona, we are a group of 37 students on our 3rd year of university full of energy to make a game that will make us proud. We already have participated in other projects with smaller groups, but this is our first time as a whole so we are looking forward to work together on these 3 moths of developing. Our goal is to simulate an indie studio and we are excited to make a videogame that we will remember with good memories.

Our team is lead by Lluís Moreu and it has been divided into three departments: Design with 12 designers and their lead Jaume Montagut; Programming, the biggest team with 15 programmers and José Antonio Prieto as their lead and finally, Art with 10 artists, one of them Nadine Gutiérrez as their lead artist.
