About Me

I'm Laia Martínez and I've been part of the art team.

During the first stages of the project, I've worked creating the concepts art of the different enemies implemented in the game.

Afterwards I focused on the development of 2D and 3D particles, used for the ambientation of the different levels and the characters attacks.
