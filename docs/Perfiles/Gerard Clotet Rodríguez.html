Combat

Combat Design

Since the beggining on the project I've been focused on the game combat particularly on the players combat.

The main goal for player combat was to feel great against the enemiess, the game requires a fast paced action, but also cooperation between players to achieve the challenges.

Rough draft combo diagram

To force cooperation between players each one complements the other

Each character has its own unique features, geralt is a based melee character unlike yenn that is Ranged Based. Characters feel unique due its combat sense, Yennefer playstyle is related to Intense crow control, and Geralt is focused on area damage.

Geralt Combos Design

Geralt Has four diferent combos each one of 3 attacks.

Light combo: more attack area but less damage

Heavy combo:more damage area but on a single target

Area Combo: this combo allows geralt to pushback enemies from all directions

Heavy combo: knockUps the enemy

Button attacks are linked to players actions, heavy button will release a vertical attack and light a horizontal one

First Iteration of combos
